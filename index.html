Frequently Asked Questions

Are these really exclusive leads?

Yes. One contractor per zip code, period. When you're in, competitors in your territory are locked out. We will never sell the same lead twice.

What if I already use Angi or HomeAdvisor?

Keep using them if you want — but understand the difference. Angi sells each lead to 4–5 contractors simultaneously. You're racing strangers to the phone. Our leads go to you only, and you get notified fast.

What types of HVAC jobs do the leads cover?

We generate leads for repairs, tune-ups, replacements, and new installations. You tell us what job types you want, and we target accordingly.

How fast do leads actually arrive?

Withfast of a homeowner submitting their info, you get an SMS with their name, address, phone number, and what's wrong with their system. The contractor who calls first wins the job roughly 80% of the time.

What happens after the free trial?

If you're happy with the results, we'll discuss an ongoing arrangement — either pay-per-lead or a monthly retainer. No pressure, no auto-billing. You decide.
